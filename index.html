About Me

I’m Harison Davidson, a passionate pencil micro-artist based in the UK. With over 8 years of experience, I specialize in creating ultra-detailed graphite sculptures on pencil leads. One of my proudest achievements includes crafting the India Gate on a 0.5cm lead in just 20.53 seconds, recognized by the India Book of Records.
